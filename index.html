unity-project

Walk around on our Mars virtual colony! Keyboard shortcuts:

  • Mouse: look around
  • WASD: move horizontally
  • Spacebar: jump / rocketpack
  • +/-: Scale up/down (to get a look at the real Mars terrain)

Walk up to a Cybertruck door to start driving it:

  • Mouse: look around
  • WS: throttle forward/backward
  • AD: steering wheel left/right
  • Spacebar: jump out of the truck

Walk behind a logistics robot to drive it:

  • Mouse: look around
  • WA: drive forward/backward
  • AD: spin the robot left/right
  • FR: move the robot arm "elbow" forward (unload boxes) / reverse (load boxes)
  • QE: move the robot arm "wrist" tilting forward and backward
  • Spacebar: jump out of robot

Models prepared by Nexus Aurora and mostly integrated by Orion Lawlor, lawlor@alaska.edu.

This is clearly a work in progress--source code is available on GitHub.
